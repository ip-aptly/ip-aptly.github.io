Patent language varies considerably from normal text!

When it comes to patents, the goal is to have an original and innovative idea. Skilled lawyers spend a lot of time making sure that the idea is completely unique and creative. This involves understanding the meaning of the idea and comparing it to other similar ones. The AI model needs to understand both technical and legal terms to do this. Patent practitioners want their ideas to be broad but still unclear, which can be difficult for the AI model because the idea is often much larger than the patent passage. Despite this challenge, our AI system is designed to handle these complexities and give accurate results.
